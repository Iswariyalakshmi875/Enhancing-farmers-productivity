Rice - Monsoon 2024

Comprehensive crop advisory

Optimal Sowing Time

Best sowing period: June 15 - July 15

Ensure soil moisture is adequate before sowing. Pre-monsoon preparation is crucial for optimal germination.

Irrigation Schedule

Water every 3-4 days during vegetative stage

Maintain 2-3 inches of standing water during flowering. Reduce irrigation 10 days before harvest.

Fertilizer Plan

NPK ratio: 120:60:40 kg/hectare

  • Basal: 50% N, 100% P, 50% K at sowing
  • Top dress: 25% N at tillering stage
  • Top dress: 25% N and 50% K at panicle initiation

Climate-Resilient Tips

  • Use drought-resistant varieties like CR Dhan 40
  • Practice System of Rice Intensification (SRI)
  • Implement alternate wetting and drying
  • Monitor weather forecasts regularly

Pest & Disease Alerts

Early detection and treatment recommendations

Upload or capture leaf images for AI-powered pest detection

Brown Leaf Spot HIGH

Fungal disease affecting rice leaves

Recommendation:

Apply Mancozeb 75% WP @ 2g/liter. Ensure proper drainage and avoid excess nitrogen.

Stem Borer MEDIUM

Insect pest damaging rice stems

Recommendation:

Use Cartap Hydrochloride 50% SP @ 1g/liter. Remove and destroy affected plants.

Bacterial Leaf Blight LOW

Bacterial infection on leaf margins

Recommendation:

Spray Copper Oxychloride 50% WP @ 3g/liter. Maintain field hygiene.
